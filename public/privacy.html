Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GeoDojo ("the app") is a free geography learning application available at geodojo.app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

What we collect

Almost nothing. GeoDojo stores your quiz progress, belt rank, language preference, and name (if you provided one) directly on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

Email address

During onboarding, we optionally ask for your email address. If you provide it, it is stored locally on your device only. We do not currently send emails, newsletters, or marketing communications. If we add this feature in the future, we will ask for your explicit consent first.

No tracking

GeoDojo does not use cookies, analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tools. We cannot see how you use the app.

No accounts

GeoDojo does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no password, and no personal data stored on our servers — because we don't have servers storing user data.

Third-party services

Flag images are loaded from flagcdn.com, a free open-source flag image service. The Nunito font is loaded from Google Fonts. These services may log standard web request data (IP address, browser type) according to their own privacy policies.

Children

GeoDojo is an educational app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the changes on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at: hello@geodojo.app
